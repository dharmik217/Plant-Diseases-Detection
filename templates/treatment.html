Plant Disease Treatment Guide

Find out what treatment each plant needs!

Apple Scab

Apple Scab is a fungal disease caused by Venturia inaequalis. It infects leaves, fruits, and young shoots, leading to dark, scaly lesions, premature leaf drop, and reduced fruit quality.

More Details

Apple Cedar Rust

Apple Cedar Rust is a fungal disease caused by Gymnosporangium juniperi-virginianae. It affects apple trees and junipers, causing orange spots on leaves and fruit, leading to defoliation and reduced yield.

More Details

Black Rot

Black Rot is a fungal disease caused by Botryosphaeria obtusa. It affects apple trees, leading to circular brown spots on leaves, shriveled fruit, and cankers on branches.
